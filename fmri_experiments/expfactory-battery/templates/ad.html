Thank you for your interest in our experiment!

This is a large experiment with many different tasks for you to do. Each time you accept the HIT you will be given a different set of tasks that will take 30-60 minutes to complete, with most HITs lasting around 30 minutes. We encourage you to repeat the HIT multiple times until you complete all of the tasks, which should take around 10 repeats. If you complete all the HITs you will receive a $10 bonus.

Most of these tasks require that you press keys and sometimes we'll ask you to respond quickly. It is very important that you follow all the instructions and never mindlessly press the keys to finish the HIT more quickly! Not only will this hurt our research, but all of the tasks are designed to take around the same amount of time no matter how quickly you respond. The tasks will be more interesting to you and useful for us if you follow the instructions and try your best on all of the tasks.

If you accept the hit you will see a start button. After you press start you will be shown a new screen outlining the tasks you will perform in this HIT.
